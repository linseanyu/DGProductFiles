Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Dnoods ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile application Dnoods. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Dnoods does not collect, store, or share your personal photos, videos, or personal data. All photo and video analysis is performed locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The app requests access to your photo and video library solely for the purpose of providing cleanup functionality. Your media never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Dnoods does not integrate with third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
